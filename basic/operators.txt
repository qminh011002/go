Logical Operators:
 ! logical not
 || logical or
 && logical and

Bitwise Operators
    & bitwise AND
    ! bitwise OR
    ^ bitwise XOR
    &^ bit clear, and not
    <<  left shift
    >>  right shift

Comparision Operators
    == equal
    != not equal
    < less than
    <= less than or equal to
    > greater than
    >= greater than or equal to
